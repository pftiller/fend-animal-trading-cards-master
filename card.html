Bottlenose dolphin

Bottlenose dolphins have the second largest brain mass-to-total body mass ratio of any mammal on earth after humans.

  • Scientific Name: Tursiops truncatus
  • Average Length: 10 to 14 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 45 to 50 years
  • Habitat: Tropical and temperate oceans worldwide

A marine mammal noted for its intelligence. They are social creatures that travel in groups of 10-30 members called pods. They mostly eat fish, but are known to also eat crustaceans. They hunt using echolocation, whereby they emit sounds and listen for echos to determine the location of prey. Their curved mouths give the appearance that they are always smiling.
